Summary

This animation was created as the final submission for a 2D animation module in college. This coursework was used to demonstrate our knowledge of and ability to utilize certain animation techniques that we had learned over the course of the module.

The animation was created using Adobe Animate. Some ActionScript coding was used for certain aspects of the animation.
