Summary

This project was the final graded coursework for my Games Development college course. We were tasked with creating a game in Unity, and included a planning phase, a development phase and a self assesment phase.

Planning

Game Description

The game was intended to be a 2-D side scrolling truck driving game, in which the player would transport loose objects through a level to recieve a score dependant on the time taken and amount of objects not lost. Originally, the game had a small backstory about a girl making a birthday card for her brother, which her brother would deliver craft materials (such as stickers and crayons) for her to make more interesting. Once the game was completed, a final part of the story would be revealed, showing that the boys birthday present was a new toy truck, which also set up the unlock for a two player mode.

Art Style

Early development art examples

From early on in the project I decided to use a simple childlike art style which used light pastel colours and was made to appear as if drawn by a child using crayon.
